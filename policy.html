PRIVACY POLICY

At Aaharkosh, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you use Aaharkosh: Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Your name, email address, phone number, and billing information. Meal Subscription Details: The meal plan you choose (e.g., breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack), meal timing, duration of the subscription (e.g., 15 days, 30 days), and location details for meal delivery. One-Time Orders: Information regarding the one-time meal order, including meal selection, payment details, and delivery address. Device Information: Information such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and browser details.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes: To process and fulfill your meal subscription orders, including customization of meal timing, location, and delivery. To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless: It is required to fulfill your orders (e.g., with delivery partners). We are required to do so by law or legal process. We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption of sensitive data and secure storage practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. User Rights: You have the right to

Access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

7. Children’s Privacy:

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.
